Lettuce

Lettuce is a popular leafy vegetable known for its crisp texture and nutritional value and is commonly used in salads, sandwiches and many other dishes.

Macronutrients include the three main components of nitrogen (N), phosphorus (P), and potassium (K), often referred to as NPK, as well as calcium (Ca), magnesium (Mg), and sulfur (S). Trace elements include iron (Fe), manganese (Mn), copper (Cu), zinc (Zn), boron (B), molybdenum (Mo) and chlorine (Cl)
